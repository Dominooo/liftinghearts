Share Your Story

Lifting Hearts is a support group for breast cancer patients and survivors. It was founded by 3 survivors who are motivated to helping others. Lifting Hearts wants survivors to share their stories for other patients to find hope during this time in their life. If you would like to share your story with us, here are some questions to get started:


  • What were your feelings when you were diagnosed?
  • What was your family and friends reactions?
  • When did you find moments of hope?
  • Who helped you the most?
  • What was the treatment like for you?
  • How did it feel to be cancer free?
  • What did you find most important that other patients would benefit from?


We appreciate your support in lending encouragement to others. Please upload your video to YouTube and give us the link below. If you have questions, contact bethanie.liftinghearts@gmail.com the Lifting Hearts Social Media Team Leader.
